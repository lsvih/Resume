About Me

I’m a second-year Ph.D. student in CS at Data Management Lab of Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University (WICT, PKU), supervised by Prof. Lei Zou. I received my M.Eng. Degree from Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS) in 06/2021, under the supervision of Prof. Tingwen Liu. I received my B.Eng. Degree from Central South University (CSU) in 06/2016.

I have extensive experience in web development, especially in frontend. I worked as Web Developer for subsidiaries of Boloni Corp, and now I’m maintaining some open source communities like juejin.

My research interests lie within Natural Language Processing (NLP), Applications of NLP and Information Extraction (IE) for Knowledge Graph (KG).
